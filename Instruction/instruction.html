Instructions

  • Click on Start Test to start the test.
  • On the test portal, test details will be shown at the top, timer below that, and question buttons below it to navigate to a specific question.
  • Click on Clear Selection to clear the response, and Save for later to review the question later..
  • To go to the previous or next question click Previous or Next respectively.
  • After selecting an option, click on Save Answer before moving on next question.
  • If you do not save your answer, it will not be considered.
  • Saved for later questions will be coloured with Yellow, and saved answers will be green coloured.
  • Click on Finish to submit all the answers and end the test.
  • If timer goes out, your progress will be saved and test will be ended.
  • To view all of your previous scores, go to View Scores tab.
  • To check answers, click on View Answers.
  • If you appear a test more than once, then your maximum score result will be saved and it can be viewed in the View Answers tab.
